About Me

My name is Finn. I was born and raised in Ireland, but I have played football in high school and college in the United States for the last 6 years.

After graduating from Newton South High school I was recruited to play for the University of Massachusetts at Dartmouth where I played for two years.

In August 2016 I got the opportunity to represent my country in a international friendly Vs. The Dutch Lions.

Last season I played my first European season in Finland with the Pori Bears, we went 9-0 and won a championship.

In September 2018 I started at corner for my country in a win over Team Belgium.
